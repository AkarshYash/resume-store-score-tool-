# 🌐 SHARE YOUR APP NOW - 30 Seconds!

Your app is running on your computer. Let's make it accessible to anyone worldwide!

## ⚡ FASTEST: Use Ngrok (30 seconds)

### Step 1: Download Ngrok
Go to: **https://ngrok.com/download**
- Click "Download for Windows"
- Extract the ZIP file

### Step 2: Run Ngrok
Open Command Prompt where you extracted ngrok.exe:
```cmd
ngrok http 5173
```

### Step 3: Get Your Link!
Ngrok will show you a URL like:
```
Forwarding  https://abc123-456-789.ngrok-free.app -> http://localhost:5173
```

**Copy that HTTPS URL** - that's your shareable link! ✅

### Step 4: Share It!
Send that URL to anyone. They can access your app from anywhere in the world!

---

## 🎯 Alternative: Use Localtunnel (No Download)

Already have Node.js? Even faster:

```cmd
npx localtunnel --port 5173
```

It will give you a URL like: `https://happy-dog-123.loca.lt`

Share that link!
